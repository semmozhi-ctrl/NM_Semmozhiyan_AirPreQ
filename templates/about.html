Our Mission

To democratize air quality information through advanced AI and machine learning, empowering communities to make informed decisions about their health and environment.

Purpose & Vision

The rise in air pollution across urban and rural areas poses a serious threat to public health and the environment. Our project aims to provide a predictive platform that empowers people with actionable insights into current and future air quality conditions.

  • Real-time monitoring
  • Predictive analytics
  • Health recommendations
  • Environmental insights

How It Works

Our system uses historical pollutant data and real-time environmental inputs to train machine learning models that can predict AQI levels with high accuracy.

Frontend: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap 5, JavaScript, Plotly
Backend: Python, Flask
ML/AI: scikit-learn, pandas, numpy
Data: IQAir AirVisual API
